Technical Breakthroughs

Addressing standard time-series flaws in watershed hydrology.

1. Zero-Lag Meteorological Alignment
Problem: Standard models delay predictions by 2 days during flash floods due to shifting inputs.
Solution: Restructured sequence window slicing to supply observed precipitation on the target day without target streamflow leakage. Yields zero-lag foresight.
2. Watershed Volume Transfer
Problem: Pre-training on massive national datasets causes raw predictions to overshoot local capacities.
Solution: Frozen CNN-BiLSTM feature extractors paired with MSE fine-tuning on local Attention/Dense layers scales weights perfectly to the local basin magnitude.
